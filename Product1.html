Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra

The Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra is a high-end smartphone with a 6.8-inch flat display, and an Embedded S Pen. It boasts a 200MP industry-standard camera with a ProVisual engine that improves color tone and detail. The phone offers up to 5x optical zoom, 50MP high-resolution camera sensors, and low-light portrait capabilities.
